The Chinese Room

A Simulation of Searle's Thought Experiment

The Experiment: You are locked in a room. Chinese messages slide under the door. You don't understand Chinese, but you have a rulebook — a set of pattern-matching rules that tell you which symbols to send back. To the person outside, it seems like you speak Chinese. But do you understand it?

Match incoming symbols to rules, then construct your response on the character grid. In LLM mode, a real language model plays the person outside the room.
